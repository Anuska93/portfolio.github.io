Projects

Infant Distress Decoder

This project listens to the cries of babies and tries to understand what they are trying to say, like if they’re hungry, uncomfortable, or need attention. It uses smart computer algorithms to learn from many baby cries and predict the reason behind each cry accurately. This helps parents or caregivers respond quickly and take better care of their babies, even when it’s hard to tell what the baby wants.
